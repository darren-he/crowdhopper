Our Story

At 9PM on a Wednesday night in 2015, we were in Midtown New York City, looking for a cool bar to grab a drink. We wanted to find a fun crowd instead of being the only ones in an empty place. Unfortunately after visiting four bars and being underwhelmed by each of them, we called it a night and split up. The next day, we started working on CrowdHopper because it was absurd that no tech tool was able to help us figure out which bar to go to.

Now, more than a year later, we have an amazing team of business, technology, and design experts working to unlock the crowds around your life. Whether you are figuring out which bar to go to on a Wednesday night, or which coffee shop to visit on Monday morning, CrowdHopper will give you the crowd intelligence you need. Happy Hopping!
